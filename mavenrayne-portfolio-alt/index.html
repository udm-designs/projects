Hello, I'm Maven Rayne Kavan.

Interior Designer ✦ Graphic Designer ✦ Fine Artist ✦ Photography

I care deeply about people. That care shows up in everything I do.

I’m an interdisciplinary designer working across interior architecture, graphic design, and fine arts. I approach each project as parts of a whole shaped by the way humans live and interact. I’m always thinking about how people move through and use spaces, how they feel within them, and how design can support, not complicate, their daily lives.

Sustainability, accessibility, and functionality are not trends to me; they are responsibilities. I don’t believe in choosing between beauty and purpose, or creativity and rigor. The work that matters most to me holds both. I’m drawn to projects that are intentional, community-oriented, and grounded in care for the people who will live with them long after the design phase is over.

I design the way I live: with intention, accountability, curiosity, and a deep commitment to the people around me.

My process is collaborative and iterative. I value honest critique, thoughtful questioning, and work that evolves through care and attention rather than urgency. I bring my whole self to what I do; not to be expressive for my own sake, but because good design is never disconnected from the person creating it or the people it’s being created for.

At the core of my practice is a simple goal: to create spaces and experiences that feel warm, grounded, and genuinely welcoming. Places where people feel safe, supported, and at home.
